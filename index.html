Why safe zones matters

Every social media platform places interface elements like profile icons, caption bars, reaction buttons, and CTAs directly over your content. When you ignore safe zones, your carefully crafted designs can disappear behind these overlays, effectively wasting your advertising budget on ads that can't deliver their full message.

For brands running paid campaigns, safe zone awareness isn't just about aesthetics. It's about conversions. Clear, visible CTAs drive action. Once you understand where each platform places its interface elements, you can design around them from the start, eliminating expensive mid-campaign fixes and wasted impressions.

What is the Campaign Builder?

Campaign Builder eliminates miscommunication before production starts. Instead of scattered spreadsheets and ambiguous briefs, you create a single, comprehensive campaign plan that specifies every creative asset needed. Platform, format type, exact dimensions, video duration requirements, and any platform-specific considerations.

Share one link with your production team or external agency, and they instantly see the complete picture: 10 seconds 1080×1920 for Instagram Stories, 6 seconds 1080×1350 for feed posts, plus all the programmatic and out-of-home formats your campaign demands. This structured approach reduces back-and-forth communications by up to 70% and ensures everyone works from the same specifications from day one.
