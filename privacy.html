Privacy Policy for SocialSafeZone

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SocialSafeZone ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website and services (collectively, the "Service"), located at https://social-safe-zone.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use the Service.

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password.
  • Payment and Subscription Information: We use Stripe for payment processing. When you subscribe to a paid plan, we receive information related to your subscription, such as your plan type and billing cycle. We do not store or have direct access to your credit card details.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your use of the Service, such as the features you use and the projects you create.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To process your payments and manage your subscription.
  • To communicate with you, including sending service-related announcements and responding to your support requests.
  • To monitor and analyze trends and usage of the Service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors who need it to carry out work on our behalf. This includes:
    • Stripe for payment processing.
    • Supabase for backend infrastructure and database hosting.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if we believe disclosure is in accordance with, or required by, any applicable law, regulation, or legal process.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with your consent or at your direction.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. Our backend infrastructure is powered by Supabase, which provides robust security features to protect your data.

5. Your Choices

  • Account Information: You can update or correct your account information at any time by logging into your profile page.
  • Cookies: Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies, though this may affect the availability and functionality of our Service.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@social-safe-zone.vercel.app
